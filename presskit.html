Dr. Brian Cohn, Ph.D.
Chief Scientist, Kaspect
briancohn@kaspect.com

Move the slider to pick the length of the bio

Dr. Cohn is a Computational Biologist and NSF Graduate Fellow with a focus on applying creative digital solutions in healthcare. In 2015, he established Kaspect, a strategy consultancy specializing in the artful use of artificial intelligence in biotechnology across various companies. His extensive portfolio spans clinical research firms, hospital networks, and biotechnology companies. With a background in computational biology and a strong foundation in data-driven biomedical science, he has contributed to the development of medical devices and AI-driven digital tools, resulting in impactful publications in prestigious journals. He is also the Co-Founder of AdventureBiofeedback and serves as the Director of Research at ObvioHealth, a clinical research organization.
